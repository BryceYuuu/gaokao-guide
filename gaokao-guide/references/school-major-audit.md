# School And Major Audit

Use this file when the user asks whether a school, campus, major, or professional group is worth applying to.

## School Audit Dimensions

Audit what daily life and future options look like:

1. 报考技术: group, seats, tuition, campus, language, physical exam, single-subject, admission rules.
2. 底线安全: campus safety, student protection, complaint handling, extreme-event signals, counselor/teacher power ecology.
3. 办学身份: public/private, independent college transition, Chinese-foreign cooperation, supervising department, industry background.
4. 生源画像: rank level, local share, gender ratio when relevant, campus atmosphere clues.
5. 校风治理: dorms, campus location, administration, transfers, discipline pressure, student-service responsiveness.
6. 学业出路: graduate school, recommendation qualification, GPA pressure, teaching quality, labs, certifications.
7. 城市生活: cost, transport, internships, regional employment, climate, distance from home.
8. 暗规则/隐形成本: allocation, relationship dependency, fees, campus split, internship access, opaque transfer rules.

## Major-In-School Audit

Separate the major itself from this school's version of the major.

Check:

- Is the major a flagship, ordinary, newly opened, or neglected program?
- Are there national/provincial first-class labels, accreditation, degree authorization, labs, discipline platforms, or industry partners?
- Are major-specific employment and graduate-school outcomes visible?
- Which courses are bottlenecks? Which direction is strong or weak?
- Are there transfer-major barriers or GPA rules that matter?
- Does the group include much weaker or unwanted majors?

## Evidence Standards

Use official and semi-official sources first:

-招生章程、分省计划、学院官网、培养方案、本科教学质量报告、就业质量报告、推免公示、转专业办法、学费/住宿公告。

Use social sources only as leads:

-知乎、小红书、贴吧、B站、论坛、在校生帖、校友群线索。

For social leads, label whether it is repeated, recent, specific, and consistent with official facts.

## School Report Shape

Start with a one-page conclusion:

- biggest advantage;
- biggest concern;
- whether it fits this student's constraints;
- what must be verified before final submission.

Then provide:

| Dimension | Finding | Evidence status | Why it matters | Risk/action |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Do not calculate a total score. Weight differs by family.

## Multi-School Comparison

For multiple schools, compare by family-weighted dimensions:

| Dimension | School A | School B | School C | Which matters most for this family |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Give a tendency only after naming the decisive tradeoff. Example: “若你家最看重本地就业和费用，A 更顺；若更看重升学跳板，B 的证据更强；C 的风险在专业组下限。”
